Neetish Sharma

Core Member

Hailing from Philadelphia
Studying Management Science & Engineering



What are you most excited for in SAS for this upcoming year?
South Asian Society motivates me not only to fully embrace my lived experience as a South Asian Pahari/Punjabi man in the United States, but to be a better American and a better man. Funny that a seemingly "foreign" ethnic background makes me more American, but I assure you, the United States is great because of its diversity—because of people like us. This is what I'm most excited about this year: bolstering this unapologetic pride among Stanford South Asians to unite together as who we are for no one else but ourselves—no filter, no assimilation necessary—and to get Stanford excited about how we're definitely a force to be reckoned with in what used to be the white man's world.
